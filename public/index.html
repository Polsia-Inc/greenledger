AASB S2 Framework

Four pillars. One report. Zero guesswork.

Australia's mandatory climate disclosure standard requires reporting across four interconnected pillars. GreenLedger generates all four, tailored to your company's actual emissions data.

01

Governance

Board oversight and management's role in assessing climate risks and opportunities.

02

Strategy

Climate risks identified over short, medium and long term. Resilience under 1.5°C scenarios.

03

Risk Management

Processes for identifying, assessing, and managing climate-related risks.

04

Metrics & Targets

GHG emissions by scope, climate metrics, transition targets and progress tracking.
