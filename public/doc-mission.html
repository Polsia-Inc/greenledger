Our Purpose

Climate disclosure is no longer optional—it's mandatory. As of January 2025, Australian businesses are required to report on climate-related financial risks, emissions (Scope 1, 2, and 3), and sustainability metrics under AASB S2 standards.

But for most Australian SMBs, compliance is:

  • Too expensive — Traditional ESG software costs €5,000-€20,000+/year
  • Too slow — Onboarding takes 2-8 weeks with consultant-led implementations
  • Too complex — Manual data collection, validation, and reporting workflows
  • Not localized — Most competitors are EU-focused (CSRD), not AASB S2-native

GreenLedger exists to fix this.

We're building the world's first autonomous AI agent for AASB S2 compliance—designed specifically for Australian mid-market businesses who need regulatory compliance without enterprise-level budgets or consulting overhead.

What We Believe

1. Climate Reporting Should Be Autonomous

Traditional ESG platforms require constant human input—manual data entry, consultant reviews, expert validation loops. This doesn't scale. We believe AI agents can handle the entire compliance workflow autonomously: data collection → emissions calculation → report generation → regulatory submission. No spreadsheets. No consultants. No friction.

2. Compliance Should Be Affordable

Enterprise ESG software charges €5K-€20K+/year because it's built for Fortune 500 companies with dedicated sustainability teams. Australian SMBs (Group 2 and 3 entities) can't afford this. We're pricing GreenLedger at $500-$2,000/year—50-100x cheaper than competitors—because compliance is a legal requirement, not a luxury.

3. Localization Matters

Most ESG tools are EU-centric (built for CSRD, ESRS) and treat AASB S2 as an afterthought. We're Australia-first: our emission factors use Australian National Greenhouse Accounts (NGA) data, our reporting templates match AASB S2 disclosure requirements exactly, and our onboarding integrates with Australian accounting systems (Xero, MYOB) and tax platforms (ATO).

4. Speed Is a Competitive Advantage

Competitors take 2-8 weeks to onboard. Group 2 compliance deadlines (January 2027) are 12 months away. Businesses don't have time for consultant-led implementations. GreenLedger gets you live in <48 hours—API integrations pull your utility bills, fuel consumption, and business travel data automatically; AI calculates emissions; reports generate instantly.

5. Transparency Builds Trust

ESG software pricing is opaque—competitors hide behind "custom quotes" and "contact sales." We publish our pricing upfront. Our tiers ($499, $1,299, $2,999/year) are transparent. No hidden fees. No per-seat charges. No vendor lock-in.

Product Strategy

Core Product: Autonomous AASB S2 Report Generator

Input:

  • Company details (name, industry, structure)
  • Emissions data (utility bills, fuel consumption, fleet, business travel)
  • Optional: Supplier data for Scope 3 calculations

AI Agent Workflow:

  1. Data Ingestion: Pull from APIs (Xero, MYOB, ATO) or manual CSV upload
  2. Emissions Calculation: Auto-apply NGA factors; calculate CO₂e for Scope 1/2/3
  3. Report Generation: GPT-4o generates board-ready AASB S2 disclosure (4 pillars)
  4. Output: Downloadable HTML/PDF; audit-ready evidence trail

Output:
