Key Competitor Profiles

1. Greenly — The EU SMB Leader

Headquarters: Paris, France
Funding: $52M Series B (2024)
Clients: 3,500+ (mostly SMB/mid-market in EU)

Strengths:

  • Accessible pricing: €5K-€8K/year entry point (vs. others' €10K+)
  • 100+ integrations: Connects Shopify, QuickBooks, cloud data, utilities
  • Industry-specific guidance: Tailored experts per sector
  • Intuitive UX: Praised for ease of use vs. enterprise competitors

Weaknesses:

  • ❌ EU-centric: Minimal AASB S2 localization; relies on generic CSRD templates
  • ❌ No true autonomy: Integrations collect data, but human review still required
  • ❌ Onboarding friction: 2-4 weeks setup + data validation cycles
  • ❌ Scope 3 weakness: Relies on external databases; not real-time from suppliers
  • User Complaints: "Lacked experience in data validation," "setup can feel complex"

Pricing Tiers:

  • CSRD Report Compliance: €5,000/year
  • CSRD Transition Ready: €8,000/year (most popular)
  • ESG Hero: €12,000/year
  • Lifecycle Analysis add-on: Custom

GreenLedger Advantage: Greenly doesn't own the Australian market; their AASB S2 support is generic. GreenLedger can undercut on price AND own the AASB-native positioning.

Positioning: "Leading platform for large, decentralized enterprises managing data complexity"

GreenLedger Advantage: Sweep abandons the Australian SMB segment. This is our market.
