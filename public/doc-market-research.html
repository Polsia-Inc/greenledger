Market Overview

The ESG reporting software market is projected to grow from $1.24B (2025) to $1.44B (2026), with a 20.1% CAGR through 2033. The broader environmental compliance software market is valued at $3.8B (2025), projected to reach $10.7B by 2036.

Australia is ground zero for this opportunity. Mandatory climate-related financial disclosures under AASB S2 began January 1, 2025. Thousands of Australian companies are now legally required to report on climate risks, Scope 1/2/3 emissions, and sustainability metrics for the first time. Group 1 entities are already reporting. Groups 2 and 3 phase in over the next two years.

Australia is one of the world's first movers on ISSB-aligned mandatory reporting, making it a pioneer market with massive early demand.
